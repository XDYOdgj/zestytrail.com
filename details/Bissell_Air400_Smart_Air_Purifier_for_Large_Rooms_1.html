Bissell Air400 Smart Air Purifier

Transform your home with the Bissell Air400 Smart Air Purifier, engineered to deliver clean, fresh air in any space. Designed for greater efficiency and comfort, this modern purifier is perfect for families, allergy sufferers, and pet owners who want to eliminate harmful particles, odors, and irritants from their environment.

Equipped with a 3-stage filtration system, including a pre-filter, activated carbon filter, and HEPA filter, the Air400 captures up to 99.97% of airborne particles as small as 0.3 microns. This powerful filtration ensures your home is free from dust, pet dander, smoke, and other common pollutants while maintaining a silent operation ideal for bedrooms and nurseries.

Key Features

  • 3-Stage Filtration System: Pre-Filter, Activated Carbon Filter, and HEPA Filter
  • Captures 99.97% of 0.3 Micron Particles and Smaller
  • Quiet Operation for Peaceful Sleep and Allergy Relief
  • Auto Mode with PM 2.5 Sensor for Smart Air Quality Management

Efficiency and Design

  • Efficient and Energy-Saving Operation

Whether you're dealing with pet dander, pollen, smoke, or everyday odors, the Bissell Air400 Smart Air Purifier is built to handle it all.
