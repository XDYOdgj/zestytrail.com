Introducing the Cuisinart CAP-500 Air Purifier

Achieve cleaner air in your home or office with the Cuisinart CAP-500. This powerful air purifier is designed to capture 99.9% of airborne particles, including dust, pollen, pet dander, and harmful bacteria as small as 0.1 microns. Ideal for medium-sized rooms up to 500 square feet, it delivers a fresh and healthy environment with two air exchanges per hour.

Advanced Filtration System

The CAP-500 is equipped with a high-performance H13 HEPA filter that traps even the tiniest airborne particles, along with two activated carbon filters for odor removal and two washable pre-filters that make regular cleaning hassle-free. This combination ensures comprehensive air purification and long-term use with minimal maintenance.

Efficient & Quiet Operation

With a 250 CADR rating, this air purifier delivers rapid and effective air cleaning, making it perfect for improving indoor air quality in your living or working space. The two-sided air intake design enhances airflow efficiency, while the low noise operation ensures comfort without compromising performance.

Smart Controls & Safety Features

Designed with user convenience in mind, the CAP-500 features illuminated touchscreen controls for easy operation. The built-in filter replacement indicator helps you keep track of when to replace your filters, ensuring optimal performance. A child safety lock adds an extra layer of security for homes with little ones.

A Reliable Investment

The CAP-500 is rated for up to 2,500 hours of use, meaning less frequent filter changes and lower long-term costs. Whether you need to tackle allergies, pet odors, or simply want cleaner air, this model is a smart choice for a healthier lifestyle.
