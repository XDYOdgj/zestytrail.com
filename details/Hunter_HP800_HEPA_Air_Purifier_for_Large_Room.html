Hunter HP800 HEPA Air Purifier

Transform your home into a sanctuary of clean air with the Hunter HP800 HEPA Air Purifier. Built for large rooms, this powerful unit provides advanced air purification to ensure a healthier and more comfortable living environment for your family.

Advanced 3-Stage Filtration System

The Hunter HP800 features a 3-stage filtration system designed to capture 99.97% of microscopic particles like dust, pollen, and pet dander. With a true HEPA filter at the core, it effectively filters out allergens and contaminants, offering superior air quality even in the most challenging environments.

Eliminate Harmful VOCs and Odors

A Coconut Carbon Pre-Filter works alongside the HEPA filter to neutralize harmful VOCs and stubborn odors, including smoke and cooking smells. This ensures that your home not only feels fresh but is also free from potentially harmful airborne chemicals.

High Efficiency for Large Spaces

Cleans up to 2585 square feet per hour with high-efficiency performance, the Hunter HP800 is ideal for spacious areas such as living rooms, bedrooms, or open-plan spaces. It's engineered to tackle air quality issues quickly and efficiently, leaving your home crisp and clean.

Peaceful Operation with Quiet Sleep Mode

Designed for restful nights, the Hunter HP800 includes a quiet sleep mode that operates at just 37 decibels. This ensures a peaceful environment without disrupting your sleep, making it perfect for homes with children or pets.

Smart and User-Friendly Design

Equipped with a digital control panel, the unit offers convenient features like 4 fan speed settings, 3 timer intervals up to 8 hours, and an automatic filter replacement reminder. It's designed to enhance your daily life with ease and reliability.

Whether you need an air purifier for pets, a large room air purifier, or a HEPA air purifier for improved indoor air quality, the Hunter HP800 is the perfect choice. Experience the benefits of clean air, comfort, and peace of mind with every breath.
