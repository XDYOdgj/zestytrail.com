TCL Breeva A3 Air Purifier

Your reliable partner for fresh and healthy air in every room of your home.

Experience Clean, Fresh Air with Advanced Filtration

The TCL Breeva A3 Air Purifier is engineered to deliver superior air purification for your living space. With a 5-stage filtration system, it removes 99.97% of dust, pollen, mold, and harmful airborne particles, ensuring the cleanest air possible. This system includes a pre-filter, True HEPA (H13) filter, and activated carbon filter, working together to trap even the smallest contaminants.

Enhanced by Anion and UV-C technology, the Breeva A3 goes beyond traditional filtration to neutralize and destroy viruses and bacteria. This makes it an essential device for families with allergies, pets, or anyone who wants to improve their indoor air quality for better health and comfort.

Smart and Efficient Air Purification

Designed to operate in your bedroom, living room, or office, the Breeva A3 Air Purifier delivers powerful airflow. Its 360° air intake ensures that pollutants are captured from all directions, while the fan circulates clean air up to 5 times per hour for efficient purification. The purifier covers up to 1230 sq ft, making it suitable for large rooms and open spaces.

Enjoy Peace of Mind with Smart Features

With Auto Mode, the Breeva A3 intelligently detects the air quality and adjusts fan speed accordingly, offering convenience and a worry-free experience. The built-in LED filter status indicator allows you to easily monitor the condition of your filters, ensuring optimal performance at all times.

Its 26 dB ultra-quiet operation is perfect for use during sleep or work, allowing you to breathe easy without any disruptive noise. The simple and intuitive design also makes it easy to set up and operate—ideal for users of all ages and lifestyles.
