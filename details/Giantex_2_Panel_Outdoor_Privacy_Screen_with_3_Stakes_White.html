Giantex 2 Panel Outdoor Privacy Screen

Transform your outdoor space into a private retreat with the Giantex Outdoor Privacy Screen. This stylish and functional design is perfect for creating a sense of seclusion while adding a touch of elegance to your garden, patio, or backyard.

Featuring two panels each measuring 48 inches wide by 48 inches high, this privacy screen offers a generous amount of coverage. The weather-resistant and durable PVC construction ensures that it withstands the elements without fading, cracking, or deteriorating.

Designed to be both strong and lightweight, the Giantex screen features a secure windproof structure with three stakes (each 20 inches long) for stability. Its no-maintenance formula eliminates the need for painting or staining, making it an easy and cost-effective solution for your outdoor needs.

With easy assembly and included instructions, setting up the screen is quick and hassle-free. Whether you want to enclose your air conditioner, trash can, or even a dumpster, this versatile privacy screen adapts to your specific requirements. It's also ideal for keeping children and pets safe from hazardous areas.

Elevate your outdoor living space with a combination of style, function, and durability from Giantex.
