Hunter HP670 True HEPA Air Purifier

Breathing cleaner, fresher air has never been easier. The Hunter HP670 True HEPA Air Purifier is designed to deliver superior indoor air quality by trapping microscopic particles and eliminating harmful pollutants. Whether it's allergens, dust, or bad odors, this purifier ensures your home environment stays safe and healthy for you and your family.

At the heart of the HP670 is the True HEPA filter, which captures 99.97% of airborne particles as small as 0.3 microns. This filter is essential for those who suffer from allergies or asthma, providing a reliable defense against airborne irritants. Complementing the HEPA filter, the EcoSilver pre-filter utilizes advanced silver nanotechnology to prevent bacterial and viral growth, keeping the air purification process efficient and hygienic.

For an extra layer of purification, the coconut carbon filter neutralizes volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and unpleasant odors from everyday household activities like cooking, smoking, and pet dander. The combination of these filters ensures a comprehensive approach to air quality, addressing both particulate and chemical contaminants with precision.

With three adjustable fan speed settings, the HP670 allows you to customize the purification intensity based on your needs. Whether you're in a busy household or looking for a gentle airflow at night, the built-in sleep mode ensures a quiet, uninterrupted sleep experience. Plus, the filter replacement indicator lets you know when it's time to change the filters, maintaining maximum performance and effectiveness.

Ideal for medium-sized rooms up to 195 square feet, the Hunter HP670 is a compact yet powerful solution for improving your living space. It's built to work efficiently, continuously circulating the air to keep it fresh and clean. Whether you're tackling seasonal allergies or just wanting to enhance the air quality at home, this air purifier delivers peace of mind with every use.
