Xiaomi Mi Air Purifier 4

Experience clean, fresh, and smart air purification with the Xiaomi Mi Air Purifier 4. Designed for modern homes and offices, it effortlessly combines powerful filtration, ultra-quiet operation, and energy efficiency to create a healthier living space.

Advanced Filtration for Cleaner Air

The Xiaomi Mi Air Purifier 4 is equipped with a True HEPA H13 filter that captures 99.97% of fine particles as small as 0.3 microns. This ensures that even the tiniest allergens, dust, and pollutants are removed from your indoor air with unmatched precision.

With its 3-stage filtration system, you can rest easy knowing your home is protected from a wide range of airborne contaminants. Whether you're allergic to dust or concerned about indoor air quality, this purifier is your reliable partner in health and comfort.

Silent Performance for a Peaceful Environment

Thanks to its ultra-quiet sleep mode, the Xiaomi Mi Air Purifier 4 operates at just 20dB of noise, making it perfect for bedrooms, nurseries, or any space where quiet is essential. Enjoy worry-free breathing without the disruption of loud machinery.

The compact design allows easy placement in any room, while the intuitive OLED display keeps you informed about the current air quality in real time. With smart home integration, you can control the purifier via voice assistants or the Mi Home App for added convenience.

Efficient and Eco-Friendly Operation

The Xiaomi Mi Air Purifier 4 is incredibly energy-efficient, consuming only 0.9KW per day. This makes it not only cost-effective for everyday use but also environmentally friendly, helping you reduce your carbon footprint while maintaining a fresh home environment.

Perfect for Every Lifestyle

Whether you're looking to improve your sleep, boost productivity in the workspace, or simply ensure cleaner air for your family, the Xiaomi Mi Air Purifier 4 delivers on all fronts. It can clean up to 517 square feet in just 30 minutes, making it ideal for busy households and offices alike.
