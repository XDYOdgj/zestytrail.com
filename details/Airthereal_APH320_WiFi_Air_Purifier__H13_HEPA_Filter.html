Introducing the Airthereal APH320

Experience cleaner, healthier, and smarter air with the Airthereal APH320. This advanced air purifier is engineered to deliver exceptional performance in any environment, whether it's your home, office, or pet-friendly space.

Advanced Filtration System

The Airthereal APH320 is equipped with a medical-grade H13 HEPA filter that captures 99.97% of airborne particles as small as 0.1 microns. This ensures effective removal of allergens, dust, pollen, and pet dander, making it ideal for people with sensitivities or allergies.

Smart and Efficient Operation

Thanks to its smart auto mode, the APH320 continuously monitors air quality in real-time and automatically adjusts fan speed for optimal performance. This feature guarantees that your home always enjoys fresh and clean air, no matter the conditions.

UV-C Light Technology

The integrated UV-C light in the APH320 helps neutralize bacteria and viruses, adding an extra layer of protection to your indoor air. This is especially valuable for maintaining a hygienic and safe environment for your family.

WiFi Control Convenience

Stay in control of your air purifier from anywhere using the Airia app. With WiFi-enabled control, you can monitor and manage the APH320 remotely, ensuring you always have access to clean air, no matter where you are.

Powerful for Large Spaces

Thanks to a high CADR rating of 188 CFM, the APH320 purifies large rooms efficiently. Whether you're in a living room or office, it ensures swift and thorough air cleaning without compromising on performance.

Choose the Airthereal APH320 for a smarter, healthier, and more comfortable living experience. With its H13 HEPA filter, UV-C light, and smart features, it's the ultimate choice for anyone committed to better indoor air quality.
