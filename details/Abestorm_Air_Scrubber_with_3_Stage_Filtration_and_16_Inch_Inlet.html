Abestorm Air Scrubber

The Abestorm Air Scrubber is your ultimate solution for clean and fresh air in any space. With a robust 3-stage filtration system, including a MERV-10 pre-filter, HEPA filter, and activated carbon filter, it effectively captures dust, allergens, pet dander, and even microscopic pollutants. This air purifier is engineered to remove 99.97% of airborne particles as small as 0.3 microns, ensuring a healthier and more breathable indoor environment.

Designed for Every Need

Whether you're working in a home, industrial, or commercial setting, this air scrubber is built to deliver exceptional performance. Its high airflow capacity of 550 CFM, combined with an adjustable range and stackable design, makes it perfect for large areas up to 800 square feet. The ultra-sturdy rotomolded shell ensures durability even in tough or demanding environments, while the compact and portable build lets you easily move it between rooms with a built-in handle.

Safety and Efficiency Combined

The Abestorm Air Scrubber features a GFCI-protected outlet, making it safe to use in wet conditions or with multiple units. This ensures reliable operation and peace of mind, especially in high-traffic or industrial areas. With its powerful yet user-friendly plug-and-play setup, it's an ideal choice for maintenance professionals, cleaning crews, and everyday users looking to improve air quality and live healthier.
