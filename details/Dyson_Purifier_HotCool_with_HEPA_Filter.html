Introducing the Dyson Purifier Hot+Cool

Experience the perfect blend of convenience, comfort, and clean air with the Dyson Purifier Hot+Cool. This innovative home appliance combines advanced air purifying technology with a powerful heater and cooler to keep your living environment fresh and healthy all year round.

Advanced Air Purification

The Dyson Purifier Hot+Cool is equipped with HEPA H13 filters that capture 99.97% of airborne particles, including dust, pollen, and pet dander. This ensures significantly cleaner air, helping you and your family breathe easier in any season.

Customizable Airflow

With 10 distinct speed settings, this device allows you to tailor the airflow and temperature to your personal comfort. Whether it's a cool breeze on a hot day or a warm draft during winter, the Dyson Purifier Hot+Cool gives you the control you need.

Smart Air Circulation

Thanks to its Air Multiplier technology, the Dyson Purifier Hot+Cool creates efficient and directed airflow, distributing purified air evenly across the entire room. This not only improves air quality but also enhances your overall living experience.

Real-Time Air Quality Monitoring

Stay informed about the air you're breathing with the built-in real-time air quality monitoring system. The LCD screen and MyDyson app provide immediate updates and insights, so you can make smarter decisions about your indoor environment.

Fully Sealed Design

The fully sealed structure of the Dyson Purifier Hot+Cool ensures that once your air is purified, it remains clean and uncontaminated. No leaks, no recontamination, just consistent, high-quality air.

Smart Notifications and Efficiency

Enjoy peace of mind with automatic filter life notifications that remind you when it's time to replace or clean the HEPA filters. This ensures optimal performance and minimal maintenance for long-term use.

Your Ultimate Air Quality Solution

The Dyson Purifier Hot+Cool is the ideal choice for anyone looking to enhance indoor air quality while also enjoying the benefits of heating and cooling. It's a versatile, smart, and stylish solution designed to improve your daily life and health.
