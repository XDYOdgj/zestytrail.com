KNKA HEPA Air Purifier

Enhance the air quality in your home with the KNKA HEPA air purifier, designed to provide clean, fresh air for your family. This advanced unit features a 3-stage filtration system that efficiently captures a wide range of airborne particles, including dust, pet dander, pollen, and more, creating a healthier living environment.

Equipped with a True HEPA filter, it removes up to 99.97% of contaminants down to 0.3 microns, ensuring that your air is consistently pure. The included washable pre-filter traps larger debris, extending the life of the main filter and reducing long-term maintenance costs.

Whether you're dealing with seasonal allergies, pet-related air issues, or just want to enjoy better air quality, the KNKA air purifier delivers reliable performance. Its real-time air quality monitor and auto mode adjust fan speeds based on current conditions, so you always get optimal air circulation with minimal effort.

For quick purification during high-pollution periods, the unit includes a turbo mode that dramatically boosts air cleansing efficiency. The ultra-quiet sleep mode lets you rest peacefully without any disruptive noise, and the optional night light adds a soft, calming glow to your room for nighttime use.

The KNKA air purifier is built with user convenience in mind. It features a filter indicator to let you know when it's time for a replacement and a child safety lock to keep your family safe. Ideal for large bedrooms or open spaces up to 2500 square feet, this versatile unit is perfect for any home looking to breathe easier.
