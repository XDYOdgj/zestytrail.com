Dyson PH03 - Smart Air Purifier with Humidifying & Cooling

The Dyson PH03 is a versatile device designed to enhance your indoor environment with three powerful functions: air purification, humidification, and cooling. Whether you're relaxing at home or working in the office, this sleek and modern appliance delivers continuous comfort and clean air.

Ultra-Pure Air with HEPA H13 Filter

Equipped with a HEPA H13 filter, the Dyson PH03 captures up to 99.97% of airborne particles as small as 0.3 microns. This advanced filtration system helps remove dust, pollen, pet dander, and harmful pollutants, creating a healthier space for you and your family.

Real-Time Air Quality Monitoring

Thanks to its intelligent sensors, the Dyson PH03 continuously tracks your indoor air quality and provides updates on the LCD screen and via the Dyson app. This means you’re always in control, with instant insights into your air’s health and freshness.

Customized Fan Modes for Optimal Comfort

Choose from three fan modes — Fan, Breeze, and Backward Airflow — to tailor your experience. Whether you need a powerful breeze, gentle purifying airflow, or focused cooling, the Dyson PH03 adapts to your lifestyle and environment.

Long-Lasting Humidifying Performance

The Dyson PH03 features a large 1-gallon water tank, offering up to 36 hours of continuous use without needing to refill. This ensures you stay comfortable all day, with consistent humidity levels in your space.

Smart Connectivity and Voice Control

Enjoy seamless control with Amazon Alexa, Apple Siri, or Google Home. The device also includes smart deep clean cycle alerts and easy-to-replace filters, making maintenance simple and hassle-free.

Experience the perfect blend of clean air, moisture, and cooling with the Dyson PH03 — your ultimate solution for better comfort and health.
